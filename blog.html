Introducing TICE: Symbolic Gravity in DeSci Finance

Beyond building a bond protocol, Q‑BOND Network is exploring novel ways to model information and trust in complex systems. One such concept is **Temporal Information & Coherence Entanglement (TICE)**, which seeks to unify quantum mechanics and gravity through memory and information. Rather than viewing gravity as a product of mass, TICE proposes that interference of memory and coherence decay give rise to gravitational effects.

The framework introduces three mathematical quantities: 

Λ = Σ (Δψ² · τ) / η  – Temporal information curvature

CI = Σ (Xₛ · 𝔇_{Ωₛ}) / cₜ – Curve Index from trust‑weighted identity flow

S_symbolic = ∫ ((dψ/dτ) – ψ_goal)² dτ – Symbolic entropy quantifying AGI coherence drift

Collectively these formulas model how information flows, decays and interferes across agents and networks. Instead of black hole entropy, the Curve Index captures thermodynamics of symbolic fields across validators and intelligent agents. In the TICE view we move from mass to memory, energy to information and spacetime to coherence.

These ideas inform our design of bonding curves, trust metrics and AI engines within Q‑BOND. By grounding financial incentives in information‑theoretic principles, we aim to create a more intelligent and aligned economy.

The TICE concept originates from internal discussions and is experimental in nature. It represents our ambition to bridge physics, information theory and decentralized finance.
